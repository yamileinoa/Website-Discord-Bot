Nymph Bot - User Commands







Help - User Commands
 Here are the commands you can use:
Economy System Commands:
!balance - Check your current balance in Uni.
!transfer @user (amount) - Transfer a specified amount of Uni to another user.
!coinflip (bet) (heads/tails) - Bet a specified amount of Uni on a coin flip.
!leaderboard - Display the top 10 users with the most Uni.
 Nymph Command:
 Include (Nymph) in your message to interact with the bot.
!listen - Nymph joins your voice channel and listens for voice commands.
 Music Bot Commands:
 !join - Instructs Nymph to join your current voice channel.
!leave - Instructs Nymph to leave the voice channel.
!play - Play a requested song.
!pause - Pauses the current song.
!resume - Resumes the paused song.
!skip - Skips to the next song in the queue.
!queue - Adds a song to the queue.
 Profiles Commands:
 !updateme - Update your member profile.
!me - View a member's profile.
!set_display_item - Show your favorite item you've obtained!
 Store System Commands:
 !store - View items available in the store.
!buy (item_id) - Purchase a specified item from the store.
 Crafting Commands:
 !recipe (item_name) - View the crafting recipe for a specified item.
!craft (item_id) - Craft a specified item using materials.
!gather - Gather materials.
!hunt - Hunt for materials.
!work - Work for some Uni.
 Text-to-Speech Command:
 !say (Your text) - Converts your typed text into spoken words.
 Timers Command:
 !timer (minutes) - Sets a timer.
